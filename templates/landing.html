11 powerful health trackers in one unified dashboard

Water Tracker

Log glasses, track daily intake, and hit your hydration goals.

How it works: Tap the + button to add glasses. The ring fills as you approach your 8-glass goal.

Calorie Counter

Log meals by type, track macros (protein, carbs, fat).

How it works: Add meals with calorie counts. View a daily breakdown of your macronutrient intake.

Exercise Log

Record workouts, duration, and calories burned.

How it works: Select exercise type, enter duration, and track calories burned. The exercise ring shows daily progress.

Medication Reminders

Never miss a dose. Track pills remaining and get refill alerts.

How it works: Add medications with dosage info. Mark as taken to decrement your pill count with auto-refill warnings.

BMI Calculator

Dynamic visual gauge shows your BMI category in real-time.

How it works: Enter height and weight during signup. A color-coded gauge shows underweight → healthy → overweight → obese ranges.

Sleep Tracker

Log hours slept and rate your sleep quality.

How it works: Enter hours and quality (1-5 stars). Track trends to optimize your rest schedule.

PDF Reports

Download professional weekly health reports.

How it works: One click generates a PDF with your 7-day stats, medications, and allergy info. Perfect for doctor visits.

Activity Dashboard

Apple Watch-inspired rings show daily progress at a glance.

How it works: Three concentric rings (water, calories, exercise) fill up as you log. Close all rings to complete your day.

Consistency Calendar

GitHub-style heat map to visualize your health streaks.

How it works: Each day gets a shade of green based on how many trackers you used. Build your streak!

Period Tracker

Phase-synced UI adapts to your menstrual cycle.

How it works: Log flow and symptoms. The app tracks your cycle phase (menstrual, follicular, ovulatory, luteal) and adapts its tips.

Emergency Card

One-tap allergy card with emergency contacts for urgent situations.

How it works: Store allergies, reactions, and emergency contacts. Access a full-screen high-contrast card instantly.
